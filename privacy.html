<h2>Privacy Policy</h2>

<p><strong>Last Updated:</strong> May 1, 2025</p>

<div class="highlight-box">

<strong>Summary:</strong> The Airline Claims Tracker is designed with privacy as a fundamental principle. We do not collect, store, or process any personal data. All processing happens entirely in your browser.

</div>

<h3>1. Information We Do Not Collect</h3>

<p>We want to be explicitly clear: the Airline Claims Tracker does not collect, store, transmit, or process any of the following information:</p>

<ul>

<li>Personal identifiers (names, emails, addresses)</li>

<li>Claim IDs or any other identifiers that could be used to access your claim</li>

<li>Detailed submission dates (only month and year are used)</li>

<li>IP addresses</li>

<li>Browser information</li>

<li>Cookies for tracking purposes</li>

<li>Any other personally identifiable information</li>

</ul>

<h3>2. How the Tool Works</h3>

<p>The Airline Claims Tracker operates entirely within your browser:</p>

<ul>

<li>When you complete the form, all data stays on your device</li>

<li>The claim card is generated using JavaScript that runs locally in your browser</li>

<li>Nothing is sent to any server</li>

<li>We use a random reference number instead of your actual claim ID</li>

<li>When you share the image, it's generated on your device and then shared directly from there</li>

</ul>

<h3>3. Essential Cookies</h3>

<p>We use only one essential technical cookie:</p>

<ul>

<li><strong>cookiesAccepted</strong>: Remembers that you've accepted our cookie policy so we don't show you the banner again</li>

</ul>

<p>This cookie is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to any server. You can delete it at any time through your browser settings.</p>

<h3>4. Third-Party Services</h3>

<p>Our site uses the following third-party services:</p>

<ul>

<li><strong>GitHub Pages</strong>: Hosts our static website files. GitHub may collect technical information as described in <a href="https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-privacy-statement" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">their privacy policy</a>.</li>

<li><strong>html2canvas</strong>: A JavaScript library loaded from cdnjs.cloudflare.com that helps generate the claim card image. This library operates entirely within your browser.</li>

</ul>

<h3>5. GDPR Compliance</h3>

<p>Even though we don't collect personal data, we follow GDPR principles:</p>

<ul>

<li><strong>Data Minimization</strong>: We don't collect any data beyond what is strictly necessary.</li>

<li><strong>Purpose Limitation</strong>: The only purpose of this tool is to generate shareable claim cards.</li>

<li><strong>Storage Limitation</strong>: No data is stored beyond your local browser.</li>

<li><strong>Right to Be Informed</strong>: This privacy policy thoroughly explains our data practices.</li>

</ul>

<h3>6. Children's Privacy</h3>

<p>Our service is not directed to children under 13. Since we don't collect any personal information, there are no specific processes for handling children's data.</p>

<p>We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The date at the top will be updated to reflect when changes were made.</p>
