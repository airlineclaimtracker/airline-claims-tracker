Privacy Policy

Key Point: SmartwingsWatch processes everything locally in your browser. Your data never leaves your device.

What We Don't Do

  • Store any personal data on our servers
  • Track your activity or collect analytics
  • Require user accounts or authentication
  • Share information with third parties
  • Use cookies (except for essential functionality)

What We Do

  • Process your claim information locally in your browser
  • Generate images on your device
  • Respect your privacy by design
  • Provide GDPR-compliant tools
  • Give you complete control over what to share

How It Works

When you use SmartwingsWatch:

  1. You enter your claim information in the form
  2. Your browser processes this information locally
  3. Images are generated using your device's resources
  4. You choose what to share and where
  5. No data is transmitted to our servers

Your Rights

Since we don't collect your data, you:

  • Have complete control over your information
  • Can use the tool without providing personal details
  • Determine what to share publicly
  • Can clear your browser cache to remove all traces

Contact

If you have questions about privacy, please contact us through our main site.
