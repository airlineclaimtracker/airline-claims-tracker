Our Mission

The Airline Claims Tracker is a consumer advocacy initiative designed to create awareness about unresolved airline luggage claims. Our mission is to help travelers whose claims have been delayed, ignored, or denied by bringing collective visibility to this widespread issue.

By empowering passengers to share their unresolved claims publicly, we aim to:

  • Create transparency around airline claim resolution practices
  • Demonstrate the scale of the problem through social visibility
  • Encourage airlines to improve their claims processing
  • Help affected travelers realize they're not alone in their struggle

Privacy by Design: This tool was built with privacy as a fundamental principle. We never collect, store, or process any personal data. All claim card generation happens in your browser only.

How It Works

Simple 3-Step Process:

  1. Create: Fill out the form with basic information about your claim (no personal data)
  2. Generate: Our tool creates a shareable claim card with a privacy-safe reference number
  3. Share: Post your claim card on social media with #AirlineClaimsTracker

When multiple travelers share their claim cards with the hashtag, it creates a visible collection of evidence showing the scale of unresolved claims. This collective approach is more effective than individual complaints and helps create awareness that can lead to improved practices.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my personal information safe?
Absolutely. We've designed this tool specifically to use NO personal information. We don't collect your actual claim ID, exact submission date, or any identifying details. All processing happens in your browser only - nothing is sent to any server.
Will this help resolve my specific claim?
While we can't guarantee individual resolutions, creating public awareness around your claim may help. Many airlines monitor social media and respond to public complaints more promptly than private ones. The collective visibility of many claims also creates pressure for systemic improvements.
Why do you use a random reference number instead of my claim ID?
Actual claim IDs could potentially be used to access your claim details, and might indirectly reveal personal information. Using a random reference number keeps your privacy intact while still conveying that this is a specific, real claim.
How can I track the impact of this campaign?
Search for the hashtag #AirlineClaimsTracker on social media platforms to see all the shared claim cards. The growing collection visually demonstrates the scale of the issue.
What else can I do beyond sharing my claim card?
  1. Continue following up with the airline through official channels
  2. Consider filing a complaint with transportation authorities in your region
  3. Share your experience in consumer review platforms
  4. Encourage others with unresolved claims to join the campaign

About the Creator

The Airline Claims Tracker was created by a group of travelers who experienced firsthand the frustration of delayed and unresolved luggage claims. After seeing how common this problem is, we decided to create a simple, privacy-safe tool to help others make their voices heard.

This is a non-commercial project with no financial interests. Our only goal is to help travelers and improve industry practices around claim resolution.
