Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 1, 2025

Summary: The Airline Claims Tracker is designed with privacy as a fundamental principle. We do not collect, store, or process any personal data. All processing happens entirely in your browser.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We want to be explicitly clear: the Airline Claims Tracker does not collect, store, transmit, or process any of the following information:

  • Personal identifiers (names, emails, addresses)
  • Claim IDs or any other identifiers that could be used to access your claim
  • Detailed submission dates (only month and year are used)
  • IP addresses
  • Browser information
  • Cookies for tracking purposes
  • Any other personally identifiable information

2. How the Tool Works

The Airline Claims Tracker operates entirely within your browser:

  • When you complete the form, all data stays on your device
  • The claim card is generated using JavaScript that runs locally in your browser
  • Nothing is sent to any server
  • We use a random reference number instead of your actual claim ID
  • When you share the image, it's generated on your device and then shared directly from there

3. Essential Cookies

We use only one essential technical cookie:

  • cookiesAccepted: Remembers that you've accepted our cookie policy so we don't show you the banner again

This cookie is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to any server. You can delete it at any time through your browser settings.

4. Third-Party Services

Our site uses the following third-party services:

  • GitHub Pages: Hosts our static website files. GitHub may collect technical information as described in their privacy policy.
  • html2canvas: A JavaScript library loaded from cdnjs.cloudflare.com that helps generate the claim card image. This library operates entirely within your browser.

5. GDPR Compliance

Even though we don't collect personal data, we follow GDPR principles:

  • Data Minimization: We don't collect any data beyond what is strictly necessary.
  • Purpose Limitation: The only purpose of this tool is to generate shareable claim cards.
  • Storage Limitation: No data is stored beyond your local browser.
  • Right to Be Informed: This privacy policy thoroughly explains our data practices.

6. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. Since we don't collect any personal information, there are no specific processes for handling children's data.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The date at the top will be updated to reflect when changes were made.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can contact us at:

Email: contact@example.com
