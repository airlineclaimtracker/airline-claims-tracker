Privacy Policy

Bottom Line: This tool runs entirely in your browser. I don't collect, store, or see any of your information. Everything happens on your device.

Who Made This

This tool was created by an individual (me) to document delayed airline claims and raise awareness about passenger rights. I built it with the help of AI (Claude.ai) to help people like me who have been waiting for claim responses.

How This Tool Works

When you use SmartwingsWatch:

  1. You enter your claim details - This happens in your browser only
  2. Your browser calculates the days - No server involvement
  3. An image is created - Using your device's processing power
  4. You decide what to share - Complete control remains with you
  5. Nothing is sent to me or any server - Your data stays on your device

Technical Details

The tool uses client-side JavaScript and HTML5 Canvas to process your information and generate images. No data transmission occurs to external servers during normal operation.

What I Don't Collect

  • Personal information (names, addresses, phone numbers)
  • Claim details or amounts
  • Photos you upload (they're processed locally)
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • IP addresses or device identifiers
  • Cookies (except basic browser functionality)

What Happens to Your Data

  • Storage: Nothing is stored on my servers - there aren't any!
  • Processing: Happens entirely in your browser
  • Sharing: Only if you choose to share your generated content
  • Deletion: Clear your browser cache to remove all traces

Your Rights

Since no data is collected:

  • You have complete control over your information
  • You can use the tool anonymously
  • You decide what to share publicly
  • No data requests are needed - there's nothing to request
  • No consent is required for data processing (there isn't any server-side processing)

Third-Party Services

This tool may link to social media platforms when you choose to share. Each platform has its own privacy policy:

  • Twitter/X: Privacy Policy
  • Facebook: Privacy Policy
  • LinkedIn: Privacy Policy
  • WhatsApp: Privacy Policy

Legal Disclaimer

This tool is provided as-is for awareness purposes. I'm not a lawyer, and this doesn't constitute legal advice. The tool was created to help document experiences and raise awareness about airline claim response times.

Contact & Updates

This is a personal project with no formal support structure. If you find bugs or have suggestions, you might find me through the social media posts this tool helps create.

I may update this policy if the tool's functionality changes. Since there's no tracking, I can't notify users directly - please check back occasionally if you're a regular user.

GDPR & Legal Compliance

By design, this tool is GDPR compliant because:

  • No personal data is processed by any server
  • All processing happens locally in your browser
  • You control all data sharing decisions
  • No consent is needed for non-existent data collection
